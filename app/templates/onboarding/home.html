DIGITAL MANUFACTURING COMMONS

Welcome to the Digital Manufacturing Commons, a cloud platform to improve manufacturing capabilities and collaboration across the manufacturing product lifecycle.

Incubated at the Digital Manufacturing and Design Innovation Institute, the DMC is your passport to the world of digital manufacturing.

DMC: MISSION

To enable digital transformation in the design and manufacturing ecosystem by helping to connect key players, analyze, and distribute relevant content on our open-source platform.


DMC: VISION

To transform the manufacturing industry by providing simple and powerful digital solutions and connecting the extended enterprise of global manufacturing.

We see the future of the DMC as millions of people using the platform daily, adding their own manufacturing recipes, examining networks in their web browsers, and using all this to learn about, research and evolve the "big-picture" issues of cradle-to-cradle low-to-zero-emission manufacturing webs.

CONNECT

+ PEOPLE: Enable collaboration and crowd sourcing of manufacturing solutions.

+ APPS & DATA: Link and integrate proprietary applications/data internally or externally.

ANALYZE

+ DATA & PROCESSES: Online tools (cloud computing) to enable multiple forms of analysis.

+ TOOLS & TERMINOLOGY: Plugable, extensible and open APIs to enable vendor neutral analysis.

DISTRIBUTE

+ CUSTOMERS: Provide large scale and reach for app creators at an affordable price.

+ PARTNERS: Ability to host apps for both public and exclusive private use.

+ CONTROLLED CHANNELS: Ability to create, store and publish app content on any infrastructure.

VALUE PROPOSITIONS

+ Improve supply chain collaboration: Collaborate within and outside of the firm in a simple, easy to use, secure cloud environment where all of a project’s files and conversations can reside

+ Improve supply chain productivity: Improve productivity of suppliers and drive down purchasing costs by providing low-cost simulation and other digital capabilities to SMMs

+ Grow supplier base: Gain access to a larger community of suppliers with digital capabilities

+ Gain new business: Inexpensively increase your digital capabilities, enabling work with new OEM customers

+ Improve productivity: Use low-cost manufacturing simulation tools to improve productivity and quality

+ Support applied learning: Leverage DMC toolchain in applied learning activities and for student projects

+ Build and commercialize your application: Leverage our infrastructure and user base in the DMC open marketplace where you can share and commercialize your own apps – without building a stand-alone software application, investing in best in class cybersecurity, and staffing up an expensive sales force


+ Democratize access and increase competitiveness: Support the democratization and advancement of digital manufacturing for U.S. competitiveness

+ Gain New Exposure: Access a new pipeline of manufacturing based clients
